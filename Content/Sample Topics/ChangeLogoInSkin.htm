Summary

Describes how to edit the logo in the toolbar of the Standard WebHelp skin.

Full Description:

The logo for the HTML5 skin can be changed in the Skin Editor:

  1. In the Project Organizer, open the Skins folder.
  2. Double-click the skin file.
  3. On the left side of the Skin Editor, click the Styles tab.
  4. Expand the Logo style.
  5. Expand Background.
  6. Click the button in the Background Image field and select an image.
  7. Save the skin file.
